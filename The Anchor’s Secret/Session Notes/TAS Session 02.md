## **Starting Location**: [[Salty Dog]]


- They meet **Frederick Wainshore**, the gatekeeper, who warns them of monsters, undead, and the dangers within.
- Upon entering, they fight bone crabs (undead crabs with humanoid skulls) and a mutated tentacle frog.
- The party finds the **Salty Dog Tavern** and discovers graffiti referencing the **Tidecutters** pirate crew and their leader, **Captain Garrick Tidebreaker**. The graffiti also mentions a mysterious pearl.
- The Salty Dog is defensible and could serve as a base of operations.
- The party investigates the area, finds remnants of previous expeditions, and notes the presence of tents and supplies not as old as the rest of the ruins.
- The session ends with the party taking a short rest and preparing to explore further.
