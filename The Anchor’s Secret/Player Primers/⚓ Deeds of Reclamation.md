## ⚓ **Deeds of Reclamation — The Achievements of New Penmaris**

_Roll a d20 or choose one that fits your hero’s story._

1. What role did you play in saving a section of the seawall during the first great storm after reconstruction began?
2. What artifact or relic did you recover from the ruins of Old Penmaris that still hums faintly with untamed magic?
3. What mysterious creature did you slay or drive off when it emerged from the flooded underways beneath the Tidewatch Ward?
4. What group of workers or refugees did you protect when a half-finished district collapsed during rebuilding?
5. What oath did you swear to the people or the city that still guides your every choice?
6. What vital trade route or shipping lane did you help reopen after the Flaming Retribution severed New Penmaris from the mainland?
7. What haunting discovery did you make along the shoreline that still troubles your dreams?
8. What dangerous salvage did you and your allies recover from a shipwreck off the island’s coast — and what secret did you find aboard?
9. What monster or spirit did you defeat that was preying on the outer tent camps during the early rebuilding months?
10. What sabotage did you uncover within the reconstruction effort — and how did you handle those responsible?
11. What strange weather phenomenon did you help investigate near the Tidewatch Ward before the city sealed it off?
12. What invention, spell, or technique did you pioneer that aided in rebuilding the city’s infrastructure?
13. What friend, mentor, or ally did you lose during a mission to secure the harbor — and what promise did you make in their memory?
14. What act of mercy or healing made you a quiet hero among the city’s refugees or craftsmen?
15. What diplomatic or trade dispute did you resolve that kept the fragile peace between the rebuilding wards?
16. What tunnel, vault, or forgotten chamber beneath New Penmaris did you uncover — and what did you find sealed within?
17. What beast, spirit, or elemental did you tame or befriend that still roams near the city’s borders?
18. What perilous experiment or ritual did you help contain before it could engulf a section of the city?
19. What secret about the Tidewatch Ward do you know that few others do — and why have you kept it hidden?
20. What honor or commendation did you receive from the city’s council, guild, or temple for your service during the rebuilding?
