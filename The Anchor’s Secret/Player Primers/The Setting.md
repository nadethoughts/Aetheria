### **Making a Character for [[New Penmaris]]**

**Who fits in here?**

- **Survivors:** Natives of Penmaris, proud and practical, determined to defend their home.
- **Refugees:** People from ruined towns, seeking a new life and a place to belong.
- **Guild Agents:** Adventurers sponsored by merchant guilds, shipwrights, or trade syndicates.
- **Seekers:** Those drawn by rumors of ancient magic, lost knowledge, or hidden treasures.
- **Protectors:** Fighters, healers, and problem-solvers who want to help the city recover.

**Common backgrounds:**
- Grew up on the docks, in the markets, or in the refugee camps.
- Served as a sailor, merchant, laborer, or city guard.
- Lost family or home to the Flaming Retribution.
- Has a personal stake in the city‚Äôs survival‚Äîfamily, vengeance, redemption, or ambition.

**Character classes:**
- **Barbarian** - Ferocious warriors who harness primal power.
- **Bard** - Skilled performers who inspire allies and wield Arcane magic.
- **Cleric** - Faithful casters who wield Divine magic.
- **Druid** - Guardians of nature who wield Primordial magic.
- **Fighter** - Hardy adventurers who excel in combat and weapon-use.
- **Mechanist** - Crafty engineers who sculpt mystic forces into items.
- **Monk** - Martial artists who harness mystical energy. 
- **Paladin** - Holy warriors who smite foes with Divine power.
- **Ranger** - Resourceful survivalists with a mystic connection to nature.
- **Rogue** - Cunning adventurers who rely on agility and trickery.
- **Sorcerer** - Powerful casters who channel raw Arcane power from within.
- **Warlock** - Supernatural casters who draw magic from Wyrd forces.
- **Wizard** - Cerebral casters who wield Arcane magic.
