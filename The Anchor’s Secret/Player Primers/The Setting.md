### **Making a Character for [[New Penmaris]]**

**Who fits in here?**

- **Survivors:** Natives of Penmaris, proud and practical, determined to defend their home.
- **Refugees:** People from ruined towns, seeking a new life and a place to belong.
- **Guild Agents:** Adventurers sponsored by merchant guilds, shipwrights, or trade syndicates.
- **Seekers:** Those drawn by rumors of ancient magic, lost knowledge, or hidden treasures.
- **Protectors:** Fighters, healers, and problem-solvers who want to help the city recover.

**Common backgrounds:**
- Grew up on the docks, in the markets, or in the refugee camps.
