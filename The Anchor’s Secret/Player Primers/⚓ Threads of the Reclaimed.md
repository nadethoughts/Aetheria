## ⚓ **Threads of the Reclaimed — Heroic Bonds of New Penmaris**

_Roll a d20 or choose one that fits your shared tale of courage and kinship._

1. You both risked your lives to hold the seawall when the tides surged higher than any had seen. Who took command, and who refused to retreat until the last worker was safe?
2. You worked side by side rescuing survivors from a capsized ferry near the harbor mouth. Whose quick thinking saved the most lives, and how did you celebrate after?
3. Before the Flaming Retribution, you stood together against a raiding band in the Verdant Strand. What bond of honor was forged in that battle?
4. You shielded a caravan of refugees from a raging wildfire during the city’s reconstruction. What impossible task did you achieve together that others still tell stories about?
5. You both received the same prophetic dream calling you to defend New Penmaris. How did you answer that call, and how did you first recognize each other from the vision?
6. You fought side by side when sea beasts breached the tide gate six months ago. What clever tactic or act of bravery turned the battle?
7. You rescued a trapped child, scholar, or craftsman from a collapsing construction site. What did that moment teach you about your companion’s character?
8. You uncovered a nest of smugglers preying on the desperate during the rebuilding effort. Who struck the first blow, and who convinced the survivors to turn themselves in?
9. You defended the city’s sacred archive or temple from looters during a power outage. What relic did you protect, and what promise did you make to keep it safe?
10. You shared command of a volunteer squad restoring order after a magical storm. How did your differing strengths make you an unstoppable team?
11. You were part of the same expedition that first mapped the edges of the Tidewatch Ward. When a creature attacked, who saved the other’s life?
12. You secured a vital food shipment for the outer camps after pirates raided the docks. What bold plan did you enact to reclaim it?
13. You helped calm a standoff between workers and soldiers during the rebuilding. Who found the words that restored unity?
14. You both stood guard over the first festival held after the city’s rebirth. When disaster struck, what heroic action saved the crowd?
15. You joined forces to escort a wounded official or engineer through dangerous streets. What did you promise them in return for their trust?
16. You aided a healer’s ward during a plague flare from the contaminated waters. Who braved the infection to bring aid to the afflicted?
17. You both were instrumental in recovering an ancient bell, banner, or relic from Old Penmaris. What symbol of hope did it become for the city?
18. You volunteered to defend a construction camp from nightly monster attacks. What battle sealed your comradeship in that endless watch?
19. You saved each other’s lives during a massive storm that nearly tore the harbor apart. How do you honor that shared survival?
20. You were handpicked by the same councilor or guildmaster for your courage in defending others. What past deed earned their faith in you both — and what mission did they entrust you with now?
