

---

## King Hargrin Deepmantle

![[King Hargrin Deepmantle.webp]] The stoic and battle-scarred king of Ironhold, Hargrin is known for his cautious wisdom and deep sense of duty. He is respected by all clans and is determined to restore Ironhold’s place in the world after the long isolation.

---

## Bramli Stoneguard

![[Bramli Stoneguard.webp]] Captain of the Royal Guard, Bramli is a pillar of discipline and loyalty. He is renowned for his unwavering defense of the throne and his reputation as a fair but strict commander.

---

## Baelgrin Deepdelve

![[Baelgrin Deepdelve.webp]] High Loremaster of Ironhold, Baelgrin is a quiet scholar and advisor to the king. He is one of the few dwarves who remembers the world before the Flaming Retribution and is respected for his knowledge of ancient lore.

---

## Thorek Ironbrow

![[Thorek Ironbrow.webp]] The royal blacksmith and master of the king’s armory, Thorek is famed for his unmatched craftsmanship. He is a man of few words, but his loyalty to the crown is unquestioned.

---

## Ingril Stonebinder

![[Ingril Stonebinder.webp]] Ironhold’s royal archivist, Ingril is known for her meticulous record-keeping and deep respect for tradition. She is often found in the archives, preserving the history and oaths of the dwarven people.

---

## Eldrin Runeweaver

![[Eldrin Runeweaver.webp]] A scholar and artificer, Eldrin is one of Ironhold’s foremost experts on rune magic and ancient history. He is a trusted advisor to the king and is always eager to unlock the secrets of the past.

---

## Dorin Flintvein

![[Dorin Flintvein.webp]] A veteran commander and military advisor, Dorin is known for his tactical mind and gruff demeanor. He has served Ironhold through many crises and is respected by soldiers and nobles alike.

---

## Captain Kael Ironmark

![[Captain Kael Ironmark.webp]] Commander of the Ironmark scouts, Kael is a survivor of the surface colonies and a master of wilderness survival. He is fiercely loyal to Ironhold and is haunted by the loss of her patrol.

---

## Rurik Hollowbrand

![[Rurik Hollowbrand.webp]] A wealthy merchant and influential member of the Hollowbrand clan, Rurik is known for his jovial manner and sharp business sense. He is always looking for new opportunities and is well-connected throughout Ironhold.

---

## Marnak Deepforge

![[Marnak Deepforge.webp]] Guildmaster of the Miners’ Consortium, Marnak is a shrewd negotiator and a driving force behind Ironhold’s mining operations. He is ambitious and pragmatic, always seeking ways to increase his clan’s influence.

---

## Maela Blackshard

![[Maela Blackshard.webp]] The enigmatic court whisperer, Maela is known for her subtlety and skill at gathering secrets. She moves easily among the powerful, always listening and rarely revealing her own intentions.

---

## Kelda Deepshiver

![[Kelda Deepshiver.webp]] A respected rune priestess, Kelda is serene and mysterious, often sought for spiritual guidance. She is revered for her wisdom and her deep connection to the ancient rituals of Ironhold.

---

## Varric Silvervein

![[Varric Silvervein.webp]] A charismatic noble from a rival royal bloodline, Varric is known for his eloquence and political ambition. He is a prominent voice in Ironhold’s court and often challenges the king’s decisions.
