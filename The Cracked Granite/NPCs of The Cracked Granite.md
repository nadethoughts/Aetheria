# **Allies/Ironhold**

## **King Hargrin Deepmantle**  
![[King Hargrin Deepmantle.webp]]
The wise and resolute ruler of Ironhold, King Deepmantle values tradition and the unity of his people. His decisions shape the fate of the dwarven kingdom.
## **Baelgrin Deepdelve**  
![[Baelgrin Deepdelve.webp]]
A trusted advisor and scholar, Baelgrin’s knowledge of dwarven history and lore makes him a stabilizing force in the council.
## **Bramli Stoneguard**  
![[Bramli Stoneguard.webp]]
Loyal guardian and confidant to the king, Bramli is the first to warn the party of corruption within the council, showing his commitment to Ironhold’s safety.
## **Maela Blackshard**  
![[Maela Blackshard.webp]]
Ironhold’s spymaster, Maela is secretive and sharp-minded. She keeps a close watch on threats both inside and outside the city.
## **Captain Kael Ironmark**  
![[Captain Kael Ironmark.webp]]
The steadfast captain of Dagger’s Sheath’s guard, Kael was rescued by the party after surviving a cave-in. Though wounded, he provides tactical advice and pushes the party to protect the refugees.
## **Varric Silvervein**  
![[Varric Silvervein.webp]]
A sharp-tongued and ambitious member of King Deepmantle’s council, Varric is always quick to dismiss rumors and downplay threats to Ironhold. The party suspects he may be working against their interests—his political maneuvering and skepticism seem to go beyond simple caution.

# **Enemies**
## **Gorvok the Maul**
![[Gorvok the Maul.webp]]
The ruthless leader of the ogre warcamp, Gorvok orchestrates attacks against the dwarves and commands the ogre forces. His presence looms over the party’s journey, even if he has not yet been confronted directly.

---
## **Female Ogre Ritualist**  
A towering ogre, her skin marked with glowing runes, performed dark elemental rituals in the warcamp. She was slain by the party during their daring infiltration, ending her attempts to corrupt dwarven captives.
## **Azer (Elemental Smith)**  
This dwarf-shaped elemental, blazing with fire, fought fiercely in the mines and later in the warcamp. The party defeated the Azer, breaking the elemental’s hold over the dwarven prisoners.
## **Ogre Guards**  
Numerous ogres patrolled the warcamp and guarded the cages of dwarven captives. Most were dispatched by the party during the rescue, their defeat allowing the refugees to escape.
## **Elemental Thralls**  
Once dwarves, these unfortunate souls were twisted by elemental magic and forced to serve the giants and ogres. Some were rescued, but many were lost to corruption, their fate a grim warning of the giants’ power.
## **Dwarven Refugees**  
Twenty-one dwarves were freed from captivity in the ogre warcamp. Wounded and traumatized, they were guided to safety by the party. One refugee was nearly killed during the escape but was saved by quick thinking and healing.
