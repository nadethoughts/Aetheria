# **The Cracked Granite – Campaign Seeding Document**

---

## **World & Setting**

### **Aetheria, Fourth Age**

- **Ironhold:** The last great dwarven bastion, carved into the Stonefang Mountains. Survived the Flaming Retribution by sealing its gates for a year.
- **Recent History:** One year ago, the Flaming Retribution devastated the surface. Now, Ironhold’s gates are open, and King Hargrin Deepmantle seeks to reclaim lost lands by sending out colonies.

---

## **Dwarven Society**

### **Clans of Ironhold**

- **Stoneguard:** Elite warriors and royal protectors.
- **Deepforge:** Miners, engineers, and industrialists.
- **Runeweaver:** Scholars, artificers, and lorekeepers.
- **Hollowbrand:** Merchants, bankers, and trade brokers.
- **Blackshard:** Informants, spies, and covert operatives.
- **Deepshiver:** Priests, mystics, and spiritual guides.
- **Ironmark:** Scouts, rangers, and border wardens.
- **Geargrind:** Engineers, machinists, and industrial innovators.
- **Clanless:** Rare, sometimes respected for independence, sometimes mistrusted.

### **Politics**

- **King Hargrin Deepmantle:** Monarch, cautious but ambitious.
- **Isolationists:** Believe Ironhold should remain sealed and self-sufficient.
- **Expansionists:** Support reclaiming the surface and rebuilding.
- **Council:** Clan leaders, guildmasters, and spiritual advisors balance the king’s power.

---

## **The Giant Wars**

- **Centuries ago:** Dwarves fought and nearly wiped out the giant tribes of the north, who were aligned with the Dark Pantheon.
- **Giants:** Evil, elemental, tribal, and devout. Their temples are now silent, their bones dust—or so the dwarves believe.

---

## **Campaign Structure**

### **Adventure Premise**

- **Dagger’s Sheath:** A dwarven colony sent to the surface has gone silent. The king cannot act openly due to political tension.
- **PCs:** Trusted, independent heroes (level 3+) sent to investigate discreetly.

### **Session Flow**

1. **Session 1:** King’s secret council → journey → arrival at Dagger’s Sheath → investigation → ambush (ogres + Azer) → rescue of Captain Kael Ironmark.
2. **Session 2:** Aftermath, clues, and Kael’s warning about betrayal and giant involvement. Stealth approach to ogre camp, observe captives and rituals.
3. **Session 3:** Tracking the warband, discovering ritual sites, leyline corruption, and the first hints of giant/dwarven rune fusion. Marnak Deepforge appears, motives unclear.
4. **Session 4:** Return to Ironhold, political fallout, assassination or framing of a loyal NPC, secret meetings, and cult activity.
5. **Session 5:** Assault on a sacred dwarven site now occupied by giants, ritual disruption, and escape with evidence.
6. **Session 6:** Final confrontation in Ironhold—trial, uprising, and the party’s choice of legacy.

## **Player Options**

### **Common Classes**

- Fighter, Cleric, Ranger, Inventor/Artificer, Champion, Rogue, Gunslinger, Kineticist, Wizard.

### **Character Creation Prompts**

- What clan do you hail from—or do you walk alone?
- What trials shaped you into a level 3 hero?
- What do you believe Ironhold should become?
- What is your stance on expansion or isolation?
- What is your relationship to the king, the clans, or the court?

## **Session 03 Starting Point**

- **Location:** On a ridge overlooking the ogre camp west of Dagger’s Sheath.
- **State:** Hidden, with a map of the camp and patrols (from Kael Ironmark), planning their next move.
- **Options:** Infiltrate the camp to rescue captives, observe rituals, track the warband further, or retreat to Ironhold.
- **Kael Ironmark:** Male, wounded but present, providing tactical advice.

## **Lore & Artifacts**

- **Leylines:** Magical energy networks, now corrupted.
- **Relics:** Dwarven artifacts with giant runes, maps of leyline nodes.
- **Giant Runes:** Evidence of collaboration or magical fusion.

---

## **Factions & Motivations**

- **Loyalists:** Defend the king, unity, tradition.
- **Expansionists:** Rebuild, innovate, reclaim the surface.
- **Reformists:** Modernize, empower clans/guilds.
- **Radicals/Cults:** Seek forbidden power, destabilize the old order.

---

## **Summary**

The Cracked Granite is a campaign of political intrigue, exploration, and looming war. The players are independent dwarven heroes, navigating the fractured politics of Ironhold and the resurgence of ancient enemies. Their choices will shape the fate of the kingdom.
