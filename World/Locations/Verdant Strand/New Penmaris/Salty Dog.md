# Salty Dog Tavern

**Location:** [[Tidewatch Ward]], [[New Penmaris]]  
**Revealed in:** _The Anchor's Secret_

---

## Overview

The **Salty Dog** is a tavern inside the sealed **Tidewatch Ward**—the ruined district of New Penmaris overrun by monsters, undead, and strange magic after the ward was devastated. The party discovered it during the reclamation; it is defensible and can serve as a base of operations while exploring the ward.

---

## Campaign reveals

- **Graffiti** inside the tavern references the **Tidecutters** pirate crew and their leader **Captain Garrick Tidebreaker**, and mentions a mysterious **pearl** (tied to the Pearlbound and the Tidecutters' goals).
- The Salty Dog is distinct from the **Sunken Shore Saloon**, which is elsewhere in New Penmaris and not in the Tidewatch Ward.
