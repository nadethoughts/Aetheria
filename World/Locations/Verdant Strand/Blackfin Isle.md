# Blackfin Isle

**Region:** Southeast of [[New Penmaris]], [[Verdant Strand]]  
**Revealed in:** _The Anchor's Secret_ (Tidecutters faction)

---

## Overview

**Blackfin Isle** is a jagged fortress island southeast of New Penmaris, bristling with hidden docks and pirate strongholds. It serves as the main base of operations for the **Tidecutters**—the ruthless pirate brotherhood led by **Captain Garrick Tidebreaker** and **Bosun Reddan Pike**.

---

## Faction use

- The Tidecutters operate from Blackfin Isle to raid shipping lanes, sabotage reconstruction, and pursue the **Pearl** they believe is hidden beneath **Tidewatch Ward**.
- The island is difficult to approach and well defended; it symbolizes the Tidecutters' ambition to dominate maritime commerce in the Verdant Strand.
