# Dagger's Sheath

**Status:** Destroyed (ruins)  
**Region:** [[Ironhold]] (southern colony)  
**Revealed in:** _The Cracked Granite_ (finished)

---

## Overview

Dagger's Sheath was a southern colony of Ironhold, established after the [[Flaming Retribution]] to reclaim the mountains. It went silent over a month before King Hargrin Deepmantle sent champions to investigate.

---

## Destruction

The colony was overrun by **giant**-aligned forces: **ogres**, **Azers**, and other elemental thralls. Buildings were shattered, stonework scorched, banners defaced, and the royal seal broken. Giant-sized footprints and runes marked the site. Survivors were taken to an ogre war camp to the west; the mines held **Cinder Rats** and became the scene of the party’s first clashes.

---

## Campaign outcome (The Cracked Granite)

- Champions (Rurik Embercloak, Hurgan Ironmark, Demlor Ironmark) rescued **Captain Kael Ironmark** and **21 dwarven prisoners** from the ogre camp.
- The colony was not reclaimed; the focus shifted to Hammergate Hold, Sentinel’s Rise, and the **Rings of Coram**.
- **Gorvok the Maul** (ogre enforcer) led the assault; **Grondar Earthcaller** commands the broader giant resurgence.
