# Time Caverns

**Access:** Via [[Luakini o ka Manawa]] (temple bridge and tunnels)  
**Connects to:** [[The Burned Wood]], [[The Swamp]]  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_

---

## Overview

The **Time Caverns** (or **Tunnels of Time and Space**) are the dungeon beneath and connected to **Luakini o ka Manawa**. After goblins broke through a cave-in into the temple, the community built a tower down to the temple bridge; the tunnels extend into a vast space that leads to the Burned Wood and the Swamp.

---

## Structure and threats

- **Drow** (priestess, rogue, warriors) and **bugbears** (e.g. **Grolmok the Fist**, **Vrekkar Bloodtooth**) in rooms such as Dining Room, Treasure Room, Leach Room, Boss Room.
- **Clues/items:** **A Fragment of a Map**, **Torn Journal Page**, **A Whispering Skull**, **Blood-Stained Goblin Runes**, **A Cryptic Prayer Scroll** (on a bugbear in Boss Room).
- Tied to the **Great Time Turner** and the hag coven (**Velkra**) and the **Ember Key**.
