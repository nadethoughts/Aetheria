# Sunken Temple of Cadens

**Location:** Beneath the boiling lake that covers [[Lumopolis]], [[The Blessed Realm]]  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (3. Ribbit's Revenge); _The War of Fractured Dawn_ (lore)

---

## Overview

The **Sunken Temple of Cadens** is an ancient temple of **Cadens** (god of music, memory, and renewal), buried first beneath the sands and later submerged when the heart of the Blessed Realm was destroyed. It lies beneath the boiling lake that now covers Lumopolis and serves as a resting place for **Llano** fragments and the **Veilcant Guard**'s hidden relics.

---

## History and lore

- The temple was consecrated to safeguard a **hallowed device** (the **Great Time Turner**) and the legacy of the **Veilcant Guard**—the party's past lives as La Pelu who hid bars of the Song of Creation.
- Four relics were placed here: the **Beacon's Blade**, **Heart of the Flame**, **Mirror of Ashes**, and **Infernal Breeze Gauntlets**.
- Ruins in the swamp (and in Oloru) show orichalcum filigree and etchings referencing the "Flame-Kissed Crown" and "Eternal Beacon of L...", tying the region to this temple and the Llano.

---

## Campaign significance (Crown)

- The party descended through the boiling lake (e.g. Brine Sharks, harmonic coral gate, musical glyph traps, Echoing Chamber visions) to reach the temple and recover a **Llano fragment** and their personal relics.
- The Sunken Temple is a key location in the prophecy of the Echoing Flame and the fight against **Vorath** and **Erabose**'s influence.
