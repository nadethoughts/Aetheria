# Ember Key

**Status:** Not yet forged (campaign threat)  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (3. Ribbit's Revenge)

---

## Overview

The **Ember Key** is a magical construct that the **Fire Walker Coven** (led by **Velkra**) is attempting to forge. It would allow them to **bypass the divine locks on the Great Time Turner** and begin unraveling the Llano's protections. If completed, **Vorath**'s influence would seep into the world as a corruptive force affecting memory, time, and truth—not as a body, but as an insidious presence.

---

## Ritual requirements

- **Toadwin Greenleaf's blood** — he is a "soul of balance" tied to the Llano
- A **cauldron** that channels the corrupted essence of the Fire Walker totems
- A **fragment of a La Pelu relic** (e.g. broken sunstone or shard of the Mirror of Ashes)
- The ritual is performed at the **Witches' Hut** in the Burned Wood
