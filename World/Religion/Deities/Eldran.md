## **Eldran, the Keeper of Echoes**
![[Eldran’s holy symbol.png]]

- **Domain:** Wisdom, Memory, Ancestors, History
- **Symbol:** An open book with a feather quill

---

### **Philosophy & Divine Role**

Eldran is the embodiment of wisdom gained through experience, the preservation of memory, and the guidance of ancestral spirits. He teaches that true understanding comes from listening to the past, learning from mistakes, and honoring those who came before. Eldran’s followers believe that every story, every lesson, and every tradition is a thread in the tapestry of survival.

Eldran is invoked by chroniclers, historians, teachers, and families who wish to honor their ancestors or seek guidance in times of uncertainty. He is seen as a gentle, patient god—never rushing, always encouraging reflection and thoughtful action.

---

### **Worship & Rituals**

- **Storytelling Nights:** Communities gather to share tales, legends, and lessons, passing wisdom from one generation to the next.
- **Memorial Ceremonies:** Eldran’s rites are performed at funerals and anniversaries of great events, ensuring the deeds of the departed are remembered.
- **Ancestor Shrines:** Small altars with books, quills, and candles are kept in homes and public spaces, where prayers and offerings are made to honor family and local heroes.
- **Journaling & Record-Keeping:** Followers keep journals or family histories, believing that writing preserves wisdom and invites Eldran’s blessing.

---

### **Cultural Role in New Penmaris**

In New Penmaris, Eldran’s influence is felt in the city’s traditions, songs, and monuments. Refugees and survivors turn to him to preserve the memory of lost homes and loved ones. Council members and guild leaders consult chroniclers and historians before making decisions, seeking Eldran’s guidance through the lessons of the past.

Eldran’s festivals are times of reflection and unity, where the community comes together to honor what has been lost and to chart a wise path forward.

---

### **Prayer to Eldran**

> O Eldran, Keeper of Echoes,  
> Guide our hearts with the wisdom of ages.  
> Let us remember the lessons of the past,  
> Honor the voices of our ancestors,  
> And walk the path of understanding and truth.  
> May your light shine in our memories,  
> And your wisdom guide us through the storms ahead.
