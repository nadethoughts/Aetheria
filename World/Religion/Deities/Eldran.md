## **Eldran, the Keeper of Echoes**
![[Eldran’s holy symbol.png]]
- **Domain:** Memory, Ancestors, History
- **Symbol:** An open book with a feather quill
- **Philosophy:** Eldran preserves the stories of the past and honors those who came before. His followers believe that memory is the foundation of wisdom, and that the lessons of history guide the future. Eldran’s rites are performed at funerals, memorials, and storytelling nights.
- **Worship:** Storytelling, memorial ceremonies, ancestor shrines, and the keeping of journals. His shrines are found in libraries, old halls, and places of remembrance.
- **Cultural Role:** Eldran is revered by chroniclers, historians, and families who honor their ancestors. His influence is felt in the city’s traditions, songs, and monuments.
