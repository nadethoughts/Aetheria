- **Worship:** Tossing coins into water, lucky charms, market day prayers, and rituals before major deals. Shrines are found in marketplaces, counting houses, and gambling halls.
- **Cultural Role:** Vessan is honored by merchants, gamblers, and anyone who relies on chance. His festivals are lively affairs, filled with games, contests, and trade.
