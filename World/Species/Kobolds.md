### **The Kobolds of [[Aetheria]]**
![[Kobold.png|300]]
Kobolds are small, draconic humanoids with a knack for traps, tinkering, and survival in tight spaces. Their mechanical aptitude and curiosity make them natural scavengers and engineers. Kobolds gravitate toward tunnels, ruins, and collapsed architecture, often thriving where others struggle.
- **Common Traits:** Darkvision, trap-making, mechanical skill, and natural adaptation (fierce or truescale).
- **Role in the World:** Kobolds are found in city underbellies, ruins, and as clever scavengers.
- **Role in Adventure:** Kobolds are perfect for navigating the dangerous, monster-filled ward and Gloamways, using their traps and ingenuity.
