### **The Humans of [[Aetheria]]**
![[Human.png|300]]
Humans are the youngest and most adaptable people of [[Aetheria]]. Their diversity in appearance, culture, and ambition has allowed them to spread across every region. Humans are driven by dreams of achievement and are found in every walk of life—natives, refugees, merchants, and adventurers.
- **Common Traits:** Versatility, ambition, and adaptability.
- **Role in the World:** Humans are the backbone of New Penmaris and many other cities, filling every role from leadership to labor.
- **Role in Adventure:** Humans excel at surviving and thriving in chaos, making them fit any role in the city’s struggle to reclaim the ward.
