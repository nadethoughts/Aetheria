### **The Orcs of [[Aetheria]]**
![[Orc.png|300]]
Orcs are resilient, powerful, and driven by exploration and survival. Their origin is sudden and forceful, and they are blessed by the god of war. Orcs thrive in adversity, making them natural adventurers and survivors in the chaos of post-Retribution Solaris.
- **Common Traits:** Heightened senses, perseverance, and stalwart resilience.
- **Role in the World:** Orcs are found in every environment, often as explorers, warriors, or laborers.
- **Role in Adventure:** Orcs excel in front-line roles, surviving and overcoming the dangers of the ward.
