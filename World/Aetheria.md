Aetheria is an ancient and vast world, a sphere of wonder and strife where divine legacies, elemental powers, and mortal ambition weave together in endless cycles of creation and destruction. ## üó∫Ô∏è [[Solaris]]: The Continent of Six Regions
[[Solaris]] is a land scarred and reshaped, defined by six powerful regions‚Äîeach bearing the marks of the [[Flaming Retribution]]:
### **[[Ironhold]]**
In the northwest rise the **[[Ironhold]] Mountains**, realm of the [[Dwarves]]. Masters of stone, steel, and magic, the [[Dwarves]] carved their halls deep into the mountains and endured the [[Flaming Retribution]] behind sealed gates. Now reopened, [[Ironhold]] balances ancient traditions with new ambitions, and cautiously engages with a changed world.
### [[Luminael]]
Beyond the **Emeraldine Mountains** in the west lies the homeland of the [[Elves]]. The fate of [[Luminael]] after the [[Flaming Retribution]] remains uncertain, its deep forests shrouded in mystery and rumor.
### **[[The Blessed Realm]]**
In the south stretched **[[The Blessed Realm]]**, once a shining monarchy devoted to order and divine grace. Now, the heart of the realm‚Äî[[Lumopolis]]‚Äîlies at the bottom of a giant boiling lake. The region is devastated, its fertile plains annihilated, and no organized government remains.
### **The Free Cities of the [[Vale]]**
At the continent‚Äôs heart lies the **[[Vale]]**, a fertile basin home to independent city-states. The [[Flaming Retribution]] brought flooding and destruction to many cities; some survived, others were lost. The [[Vale]] is fractured, with refugees and survivors forging new alliances and rivalries.
### **The [[Shimmering Shores]]**
Along the east ran the **[[Shimmering Shores]]**, a cosmopolitan stretch of coastline known for its harbors and ports. Half the region was destroyed and sunk into the ocean; the surviving land is now coastal, with ports and settlements clinging to the new shoreline.
### **[[Verdant Strand]]**
To the north lies the **[[Verdant Strand]]**, a region of rocky cliffs, pine forests, and brisk seas. The Retribution battered its towns and harbors, and the region is still in the process of rebuilding. Survivors gather in places like [[New Penmaris]], but prosperity remains a distant hope.
