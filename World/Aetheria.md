Aetheria is an ancient and vast world, a sphere of wonder and strife where divine legacies, elemental powers, and mortal ambition weave together in endless cycles of creation and destruction. Its lands are shaped by both natural forces and the scars of divine conflict, and its people endure within landscapes as varied as soaring mountain ranges, enchanted forests, storm-lashed coasts, and shattered plains.

## üó∫Ô∏è Solaris: The Continent of Six Regions

Solaris is a land scarred yet unbroken, defined by six powerful regions.

### **Ironhold**

In the northwest rise the **Ironhold Mountains**, realm of the Dwarves. Masters of stone, steel, and magic, the Dwarves carved their halls deep into the mountains and endured the Flaming Retribution behind sealed gates. Now reopened, Ironhold balances ancient traditions with new ambitions.

### **Luminael**

Beyond the **Emeraldine Mountains** in the west lies the psionic homeland of the Elves. In the deep forests of **Luminael**, Elves wield power not through sorcery but through **psionics**‚Äîdiscipline of the mind, spirit, and perception. They are guardians of secrets older than the Retribution.

### **The Blessed Realm**

In the south stretches the **Blessed Realm**, a shining monarchy devoted to order and divine grace. Its fertile **Sunlit Plains** provide food and trade for much of Solaris, and its rulers hold themselves as exemplars of stability amid chaos.

### **The Free Cities of the Vale**

At the continent‚Äôs heart lies the **Vale**, a fertile basin home to independent city-states. Here, diverse peoples gather in a tapestry of rivalries, alliances, and innovations. The Vale is the **melting pot of Solaris**, and many great adventures begin in its markets, guild halls, and courts.

### **The Shimmering Shores**

Along the east runs the **Shimmering Shores**, a cosmopolitan stretch of coastline known for its harbors and ports. Trade flows here from across Aetheria, making it a place of wealth, opportunity, and peril as merchant barons and sailors vie for dominance.

### **Verdant Strand**

To the north lies the **Verdant Strand**, a region of rocky cliffs, pine forests, and brisk seas. Though battered by the Retribution, its towns and harbors were rebuilt swiftly, driven by resilience and ambition. The Strand is a region of merchants, sailors, and adventurers, its people hardened by storm and stone.
