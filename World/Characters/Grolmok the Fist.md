# Grolmok the Fist

**Race:** Bugbear  
**Role:** Antagonist in the tunnels beneath [[Luakini o ka Manawa]]  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (2. Aftermath)

---

## Overview

Grolmok the Fist is one of the bugbear leaders encountered in **The Tunnels of Time and Space**—the dungeon reached through the cave-in in the Luakini o ka Manawa temple. The dungeon connects to the temple, and goblin forces broke through into the temple from below.

---

## Campaign context

- **A Cryptic Prayer Scroll** is found on the corpse of a bugbear in the Boss Room of the dungeon.
- Grolmok operates alongside **Vrekkar Bloodtooth** (another bugbear) in the service of the forces driving the goblin incursions and the hunt for the **Great Time Turner**.
