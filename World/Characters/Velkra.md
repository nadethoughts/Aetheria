# Velkra

**Role:** Hag Matron, leader of the **Fire Walker Coven**  
**Location:** [[The Burned Wood]] (Witches' Hut)  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (arcs 2–3)

---

## Overview

Velkra is the tall, hunched leader of a coven of hags operating in the Burned Wood. She and her sisters perform rituals tied to the **Ember Key**—a construct that would allow them to bypass divine locks on [[The Great Time Turner]] and unravel protections on the [[Llano]].

---

## The Ember Key ritual

- The hags are not resurrecting [[Vorath the Betrayer]] directly; they **forge the Ember Key** using:
  - **Toadwin Greenleaf's** blood (he is a "soul of balance" tied to the Llano)
  - A cauldron that channels corrupted essence of the Fire Walker totems
  - A fragment of a La Pelu relic (e.g. broken sunstone or shard of the Mirror of Ashes)
- If the ritual completes, Vorath's influence can seep into the world as a **corruptive force** affecting memory, time, and truth.
- The **Witches' Hut** is a twisted, vine-choked hut built atop ruins of an ancient La Pelu shrine; the cauldron there is the ritual focus.

---

## Combat / encounter

- **Velkra** — ritual caster (Hag Matron)
- Supported by **Fire Walker Goblin Brutes** and a **Goblin Hexer**; the cauldron pulses with fire/psychic damage in a 10-ft radius unless disabled.
- Objectives: rescue Toadwin, disable or destroy the cauldron, defeat or drive off the hags.
