# Reddan Pike

**Role:** Bosun (quartermaster and enforcer) of the **Tidecutters**  
**Faction:** [[The Verdant Strand/Tidecutters]]  
**Revealed in:** _The Anchor's Secret_

---

## Overview

Reddan Pike is the Tidecutters' quartermaster and enforcer, feared for his cruelty and skill with blade and rope. He ensures crew loyalty through a mix of terror and reward. He operates under **Captain Garrick Tidebreaker** and is referenced in campaign notes and faction lore as a key figure in the pirate brotherhood.
