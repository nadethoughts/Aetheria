# Hurgan, Dwarven Scout Ranger

Ironmark Clan • “The Night Watch Sentinel” • Hero of The Cracked Granite
![[Hurgan Portrait.jpg]]
## Appearance & Gear

- Male dwarf, mid-30s (human equivalent), powerful and broad-shouldered (~4'10"–5'0")
- Long, blonde, windblown hair; full, practical beard; tanned, weathered skin
- Warm, intense eyes; geometric frostwalker tattoo visible on forearm or neck
- Medium hardshell surki carapace armor (rune-etched, fur-lined, battle-worn)
- Dual-wields a dwarven war axe and a warhammer; clan dagger at belt; sign of sigils among his gear
- Always depicted scanning the horizon, vigilant and ready for danger

## Background & Personality

Born to the Night Watch, Hurgan learned vigilance before he could speak, standing guard in the wind-scoured peaks above Ironhold. He is a master of survival, renowned for his endurance, resistance to poison, and uncanny ability to spot danger in the dark. Hurgan quietly opposes the law that firstborn sons must follow their father’s profession, wishing for his physically impaired son to choose his own path. He is respected as a guardian and guide—loyal, rugged, and ever watchful.md). 
## Role in The Cracked Granite

### Key Events & Achievements

- **Sentinel of the North:** Hurgan’s vigilance was crucial in detecting the first signs of the giant incursion and the elemental corruption spreading through the mountains. His knowledge of the land allowed the party to navigate treacherous passes and avoid ambushes.
- **Rescue and Evacuation:** Hurgan led the infiltration of the ogre camp, using his survival skills to guide the party and the rescued dwarven captives safely through enemy territory and back to Ironhold.
- **The Rings of Coram:** At the ancient monument, Hurgan’s keen senses helped the party uncover hidden threats and disrupt the elemental ritual, preventing the creation of more thralls.
- **Defender in the Dark:** During a cave-in caused by political infighting, Hurgan took a crossbow bolt meant for Demlor, saving his comrade’s life. The two commemorated the event with matching tattoos.
- **Tactical Mastery:** Hurgan and Rurik used stealth to flank and eliminate goblin leadership before they could collapse a hall, and he fought side-by-side with Demlor in the Battle of the Drunken Cave, defeating a drunken orc scout team.
- **Recovery of Relics:** Hurgan recovered the Lost Key of the Ironmark Clan, returning it to the temple archives, and discovered a statue of a lost dwarven goddess with Rurik, hinting at forgotten aspects of dwarven faith.
- **Scout of Vargrom:** After the campaign, Hurgan volunteered to scout the newly discovered island of Vargrom, opening the door for future exploration and intelligence gathering for Ironhold.

### Relationships

- **Demlor:** Saved Demlor’s life during a cave-in; their bond was sealed with commemorative tattoos. The two fought together in pivotal battles, forging a deep trust.
- **Rurik:** Collaborated on tactical operations, including stealth attacks and the recovery of lost relics.

### Legacy

Hurgan emerged from The Cracked Granite as a hero of Ironhold and a symbol of vigilance and selfless service. His actions saved lives, preserved ancient relics, and provided Ironhold with the intelligence needed to survive the coming war. As a scout and sentinel, Hurgan continues to defend his people, ever watchful for the next shadow on the horizon)
