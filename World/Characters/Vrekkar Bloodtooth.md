# Vrekkar Bloodtooth

**Race:** Bugbear  
**Role:** Antagonist in the tunnels beneath [[Luakini o ka Manawa]]  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (2. Aftermath)

---

## Overview

Vrekkar Bloodtooth is a bugbear leader in **The Tunnels of Time and Space**—the dungeon beneath the Luakini o ka Manawa temple. The goblin incursions that broke into the temple from below are tied to these bugbears and the hag coven (e.g. **Velkra**) seeking the **Great Time Turner**.
