# Grul'Mog the Deep Belly (Froghemoth)

**Type:** Froghemoth (massive amphibious horror)  
**Region:** [[The Swamp]], near [[Oloru]] ([[Namar'Zul]] territory)  
**Revealed in:** _Crown of the Immortals_ (3. Ribbit's Revenge)

---

## Overview

Grul'Mog is a froghemoth—a towering, bulbous creature of the steaming swamps. The Namar'Zul and travelers in the region know the waters as home to "wrathful frogmen" and dangerous monsters; Grul'Mog represents one of the most fearsome of these.

---

## Campaign context

- The party encountered Grul'Mog during the journey through the swamp (Ribbit's Revenge). The swamp is covered with boardwalks to avoid the scalding water; giant flora, insects, undead, and creatures like Grul'Mog make travel perilous.
- **Namar'Zul** lore (in World) lists Grul'Mog alongside the Fire Walker Tribe, hag mistresses, and undead from ruined Lumopolis as threats in the volatile swamp.
