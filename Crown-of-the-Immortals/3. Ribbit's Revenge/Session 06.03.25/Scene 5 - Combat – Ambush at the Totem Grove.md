### ⚔️ Scene 5: Combat – Ambush at the Totem Grove (45–60 min)

**Setup:** As the party investigates the totem, they are ambushed by Fire Walker forces.

**Enemies:**

- 3 Fire Walker Goblins (melee/ranged)
- 1 Goblin Hexer (illusion/fire magic)
- 1 Swamp Wight (undead Tripkee warrior, corrupted by Velkra)

**Terrain:**

- Boardwalks over boiling water
- Steam vents (concealment zones)
- Totem pulses with dark energy (can be disabled)

**Objectives:**

- Survive the ambush
- Capture the Hexer (optional)
- Discover a ritual map fragment or whispered coordinates to the Fire Walker camp
