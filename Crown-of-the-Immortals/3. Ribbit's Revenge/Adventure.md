# Description
The party is continuing their investigation into the mysteries of the [[Time Caverns]].  They left the time caverns to enter into a swamp, after chatting with a village of [[Goblin#The Burned Wood|Goblins of the Burned Woods]] that are just looking to make a new life. The Goblins Warned them of Undead, Wrathful Frogmen and Dangerous monsters and plants. The Swamp itself is covered with boardwalks to keep travelers out of the steaming hot water.
