## 📈 Level-band progression (Pathfinder 2e style)

Use these as a loose guide for **scope and stakes**, not strict level gates.

| Tier | Level range | Tone | Example stakes |
|------|-------------|------|----------------|
| **1 – Local heroes** | 1–4 | Town/region threats; survival; first relics | Goblin raids, kobold dungeon, ward skirmishes |
| **2 – Champions** | 5–8 | Faction-level; ancient secrets; first Llano/Veilcant payoffs | Sunken Temple, hag coven, giant/ogre war, ward reclamation |
| **3 – Continental** | 9–14 | Cross-region; divine/cosmic stakes | More Llano fragments, Erabose cults, Ironhold/Vale politics |
| **4 – World-shapers** | 15–20 | Fate of Aetheria; Llano whole or shattered; Raythelion vs Erabose | Final fragments, Vorath, Time Turner, 4th Age definition |

## ✅ How to use this doc

1. **Before a new arc** — Read “Unresolved threads” and “Future arc brainstorm”; pick 1–2 threads to advance.
2. **After a session** — Check off resolved threads; add new ones under the right campaign.
3. **When stuck** — Use “World-level hooks” and “Level-band progression” to pick a region or tier and brainstorm from there.
4. **When linking campaigns** — Use “Cross-campaign links” so events in one game can subtly affect another.
