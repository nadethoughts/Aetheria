## Session 24 Summary

### ⚔️ The Hag Battle — Concluded

The session picked up mid-boss fight against the **hag coven** — specifically **Velcra** (a nightmare hag) and her **nightmare horse** (a fiend-type beast). The party was in rough shape coming in.

The first hag was killed when she tried to flee across the river, eating reactive strikes from two party members as she went down. Velcra proved far more tenacious, landing a **Sleep spell** on Karsten and trying to hit Cahir with a **dominate** effect (he resisted it — barely). Alwor landed a clutch **True Name + Slow** combo to shut down Velcra's action economy, and the party used **Calm Emotions** to freeze both Velcra and the nightmare simultaneously. Samir finished Velcra off with a devastating **41-point psychic blast** that caved her head in.

With Velcra dead, the party briefly attempted to **tame the nightmare horse** (DC 30 — diplomacy, survival, or occultism). Karsten made a real run at it but fell short, and the nightmare bolted out of the stable.

---

### 🐸 Toadwin Rescued

The party searched the hag's hut and found **Toadwin** hogtied over a flaming cauldron full of golden liquid. They cut him down. He was shaken but grateful, and came across as notably wiser and more grounded than his brother Prince Troubadour. He confirmed he'd been jumped by goblins and brought here, unaware of his exact location. He was eager to reunite with his brother and get home.

---

### 📖 Loot & Revelations

Two significant finds in the hut:

**Velcra's Diary** — A dream journal and instruction log. Most importantly, it confirmed that Velcra was **in direct communication with Vorath**, taking orders from him. He had been orchestrating the goblin attacks through her. The diary also revealed that **Vorath had a long-term plan that was disrupted by an unknown event** (referred to cryptically as being caused by "a fool"). Because of this setback, Vorath is now trying to **use the Luakini o ka Manawa (the Time Turner temple) to reverse time** and undo whatever went wrong.

**The Black Sphere** — A dark, polished orb the size of a small cantaloupe radiating dark magic. Samir used _Read Psychometric Resonance_ on it and picked up Velcra's emotional imprint: a twisted mixture of fear, reverence, and something like obsessive longing directed at its true owner. The party theorized it's connected to **Vorath** — possibly a sending stone or scrying device. As they exited the cave, the sphere **flashed and they all heard a voice in their minds**: _"You will not get in my way again. I will stop you this time before you get powerful enough to defeat me."_ — and then they all woke up in a tavern.

---

### 🎲 Meta: System Change Incoming

The GM wrapped the session by announcing the campaign is **transitioning away from Pathfinder 2e** to a new system. Two options were discussed — **Nimble** (fast-playing, similar to 5e) and **Draw Steel** (heroic fantasy, everyone hits every turn, power escalates through the adventuring day). The group leaned toward **Draw Steel**. Characters will carry over in spirit, with pre-generated equivalents: Cahir → human Sensor (paladin), Karsten → dwarven Fury (barbarian), Samir/Jason → halfling Elementalist, Alwor/Ramsey → wood elf Troubadour.

---

### 🧩 Key Takeaways

- **Vorath is actively working to control or use the Great Time Turner** under Avaotaon — the hag coven was building a key (the "Ember Key") requiring royal blood to do it.
- **Something derailed Vorath's plans** years ago and he wants to undo it via time manipulation.
- The **black sphere is likely a direct line to Vorath** — the party has it but it's dangerous.
- **500 XP awarded** — party is close to level 6.
